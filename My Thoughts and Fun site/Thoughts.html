Speaking skills

Michael Kosta's mother used to train speaking skills to her kids by throwing them a ball while asking something and the kid is not allowed to speak until it throws the ball back with another question. I really believe most of us would benefit from better speaking skills. This is the time when I am grateful for my pedagogy background.
